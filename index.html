    Our Story

    BlewMinds is a new age, radical & transformation-thinking eclectic boutique full services StoryTelling Consulting organization. We work in the meaningful spaces of Diversity Hiring, Inclusion Coaching, Employer Brand of Choice, Marketing Communications & Organization Branding, Primary & Secondary Market Research, Leadership Coaching, HR Consulting, Business Coaching, Learning, Capability Development, Technology Consulting, Executive Presence, StoryTelling & Personal Branding.

    With our focus on Diversity Hiring & Inclusion, we hope to fulfill our purpose towards helping others, enabling their progress & creating equal opportunities for all across our community. From hiring in the Diversity space, enabling LGBTQ+ communities, sensitive training in areas of unconscious bias, to coaching & enabling ‘diverse individuals’, this arena drives our passion towards ‘Inclusion’. We offer end to end services on making people & organizations more productive. Our Vision is to touch & transform 5B lives by Year 2030.
